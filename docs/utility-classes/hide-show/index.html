Hide & Show

Usage

  • *:hide
  • *:show
  • *:show-inline

<div class="line">
  <div class="x:scol-3">
    <p class="x:hide s:show">Lorem ipsum</p>
  </div>
  <div class="x:scol-3">
    <p class="s:hide m:show">Lorem ipsum</p>
  </div>
  <div class="x:scol-3">
    <p class="m:hide l:show">Lorem ipsum</p>
  </div>
  <div class="x:scol-3">
    <p class="l:hide x:show">Lorem ipsum</p>
  </div>
</div>

The columns are of the inline-block type, it is important to use *:show-inline in columns or elements to be displayed again and require to keep or set the display: inline-block property.


<div class="line">
  <div class="x:scol-3 x:hide s:show-inline">1</div>
  <div class="x:scol-3 s:hide m:show-inline">2</div>
  <div class="x:scol-3 m:hide l:show-inline">3</div>
  <div class="x:scol-3 l:hide">4</div>
</div>
